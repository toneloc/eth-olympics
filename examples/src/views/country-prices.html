Country Prices 🌎 🇰🇷

Each country is assigned a price based on how well they are predicted to do in the Olympics. Country prices are recorded in the smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain.

Prices are determined according to the share of the medal-points they expected earn: 10 points for Gold, 6 points for Silver, 3 Points for bronze, according to data below. Data was averaged from medal count predictions from several news agencies.
